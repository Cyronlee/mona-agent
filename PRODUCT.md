# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Product teams — PMs, developers, and designers — managing the full product lifecycle. They connect context sources (files, apps, instructions), and AI agents continuously analyze this context to surface relevant suggestions. Their primary job: making product decisions faster with an AI co-pilot that works alongside them, not as a passive search box.

## Product Purpose

Mona is an AI workspace co-pilot that auto-generates actionable suggestions, questions, and confirmations by analyzing working activities. Users confirm suggestions to auto-create tasks handled by multiple AI agents in parallel. Five workspace tabs (Preview, PRD, Design, Features, Code) give teams a single view across the full product lifecycle.

## Brand Personality

Confident, capable, focused. The interface speaks with the calm authority of a tool that knows what it's doing — not chatty, not coy, not trying to be your friend. The monkey mascot adds character without undermining credibility.

## Anti-references

- **Terminal / hacker aesthetic**: no neon-on-dark, no green-on-black, no retro CLI styling
- **Overly playful / gamified tools**: no confetti, no cartoon panels, no Trello-esque whimsy
- **Cluttered enterprise dashboards**: no Jira-style dense panels, no Azure DevOps complexity

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — The tool demonstrates the AI capabilities it offers. Every suggestion is earned, not noise.
2. **Show, don't tell** — Suggestions appear in context, not as abstract notifications. Results speak louder than explanations.
3. **Focus over features** — One task at a time, clear hierarchy, no panel sprawl. The workspace earns its tabs.
4. **Capable, not chatty** — The AI is a teammate, not a personality. Tone is direct, precise, and useful.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No specific compliance target. This is a demo/prototype; basic best practices apply.
