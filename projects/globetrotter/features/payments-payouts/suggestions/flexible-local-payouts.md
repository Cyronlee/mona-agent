## Context

Locals, especially those traveling or residing internationally, may have diverse preferences and limitations regarding how they can receive payments. Relying on a single or very limited set of payout options can be inconvenient, expensive, or even impossible for some, hindering Local acquisition and retention.

## Suggested Action

Integrate with additional payout providers to offer a broader range of options. This could include:
*   **Direct Bank Transfers:** Support for ACH (US), SEPA (Europe), and other local bank transfer methods where feasible.
*   **Digital Wallets:** (e.g., Wise, Payoneer, local popular digital wallets).
*   **Faster Payouts:** Offer expedited payout options for a small fee.

This requires integration with payout APIs and updating the Local's profile settings to allow them to select and manage their preferred payout method.

## Impact Assessment

**Positive:**
*   **Local Acquisition & Retention:** Attracts more Locals by accommodating their payment preferences.
*   **User Satisfaction:** Improves the experience for Locals receiving their earnings.
*   **Global Accessibility:** Caters to a wider international user base.

**Negative:**
*   **Development Effort:** Integration with multiple financial services can be complex.
*   **Operational Overhead:** Managing multiple payout methods and ensuring compliance.
*   **Fees:** Each payout method might have its own transaction fees, which need to be managed and communicated.
