## Overview

This feature encompasses all aspects of a user's journey from initial sign-up to managing their personal and operational profiles on the Globetrotter platform. It includes registration methods, different profile fields for Shoppers and Locals, and mechanisms for users to update their information.

## Design Constraints

*   Must comply with data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
*   Profile data should be easily editable by the user.
*   Secure handling of sensitive information (e.g., payment methods, payout details).
*   Profile creation should be intuitive and not overly burdensome.

## Open Questions

*   What specific third-party identity verification services should be considered for Locals?
*   Should email verification be mandatory for all users upon registration?
*   What are the minimum required fields for Shopper and Local profiles to allow them to engage with the platform?
