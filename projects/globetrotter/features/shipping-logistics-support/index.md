## Overview

This feature aims to alleviate the burden of international shipping for Locals. It includes integrations with shipping carriers for real-time rates and label generation, robust package tracking capabilities, and comprehensive resources on customs, duties, and restricted items to ensure smooth and compliant deliveries.

## Design Constraints

*   Shipping cost estimates must be as accurate as possible to avoid disputes.
*   Integration with multiple major international carriers is desirable.
*   Customs information should be easily accessible and regularly updated.
*   Package tracking should provide granular updates and be reliable.
*   Consideration for different packaging types and dimensions.

## Open Questions

*   Which specific shipping carriers will be integrated first (e.g., DHL, FedEx, UPS, national postal services)?
*   How will duties and taxes be estimated and handled? Should they be collected upfront or paid upon delivery?
*   What level of insurance coverage will be offered or recommended for shipments?
*   How will returns and damaged goods during shipping be managed within the logistics framework?
