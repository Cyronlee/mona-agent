## Context

International shipping is fraught with complexities related to customs declarations, import duties, taxes, and country-specific restrictions on certain goods. Locals, particularly those new to international shipping, often lack the expertise to navigate these requirements, leading to shipping delays, unexpected costs, or even legal issues. Providing clear guidance is crucial for smooth operations.

## Suggested Action

Create a comprehensive, easily searchable knowledge base or an integrated guidance module within the shipping workflow. This resource should cover:
*   **Customs Declaration:** How to accurately fill out customs forms (e.g., CN22/CN23).
*   **Duties & Taxes:** General information on how duties and taxes are calculated and who is responsible for payment (Shopper/Local).
*   **Restricted/Prohibited Items:** A searchable database of items that are restricted or banned from import/export for common country pairs.
*   **Documentation:** Required documentation for specific types of goods or high-value items.

This will involve research into international shipping regulations and regular updates to the content.

## Impact Assessment

**Positive:**
*   **Reduced Shipping Issues:** Fewer packages held up in customs or returned due to non-compliance.
*   **User Confidence:** Empowers Locals to ship confidently, knowing they have accurate information.
*   **Legal Compliance:** Helps the platform and users comply with international trade laws.
*   **User Experience:** Reduces stress and frustration for Locals.

**Negative:**
*   **Research & Maintenance Effort:** Requires continuous effort to research and keep regulatory information up-to-date.
*   **Complexity:** International regulations are vast and can be difficult to simplify universally.
*   **Liability:** While providing guidance, the platform might still face questions about liability if information is misunderstood or regulations change rapidly.
