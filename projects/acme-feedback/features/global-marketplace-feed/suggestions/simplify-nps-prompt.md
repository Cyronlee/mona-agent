## Context

NPS response rates on the feedback prompt dropped from 34% to 18% over the last quarter. User interviews indicate the 0–10 scale feels ambiguous and time-consuming on mobile.

## Suggested Action

Replace the NPS prompt (0–10 scale) with a 1–5 star rating widget. Keep the optional free-text follow-up but make it collapsible.

## Impact Assessment

Based on A/B test data from a comparable product, a 1–5 rating typically yields a 20–30% lift in response rates.
