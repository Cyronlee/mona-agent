## Context

During a Zoom call on 2026-05-28, Henry Howarth (PM, Acme APAC) noted that power users frequently ask for a way to search across the entire catalogue rather than relying on the curated feed alone. He also mentioned that the current category filter is too coarse — users want sub-category filtering.

## Suggested Action

1. Promote the search icon to a full-width search bar above the feed on the Explore tab.
2. Extend the filter panel with a sub-category level (e.g., Electronics → Phones, Laptops).

## Impact Assessment

Estimated to reduce average time-to-find by ~40% based on session recording analysis.
